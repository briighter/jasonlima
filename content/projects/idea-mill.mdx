Idea Mill monitors news from major publishers — TechCrunch, Bloomberg, Reuters, WSJ, and more — and uses AI to extract the business opportunities buried inside.

The core loop: pick an article, get a ranked list of problems it surfaces, and see AI-generated business ideas for each one. Each idea includes a market opportunity breakdown, who's affected, and why it matters right now.

## The insight

Every newsworthy shift creates friction somewhere. Hospitals overcrowding, doctor shortages, regulatory changes — these aren't just headlines, they're founder prompts. Idea Mill makes that pattern explicit and repeatable.

Built for entrepreneurs and analysts who want to stay ahead of industry shifts without spending hours on manual research.
