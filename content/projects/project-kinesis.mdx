Project Kinesis is a mobile experiment in human intuition — specifically, whether people can train themselves to predict Zener card symbols above chance through repeated practice.

The app shows a series of cards (circle, cross, waves, square, star) and asks users to predict each symbol before it's revealed. It tracks accuracy over sessions and lets users see whether their intuition is genuinely improving or regressing to the mean.

## Why I built it

More of a question than a product: can you train a skill that might not exist? The app is deliberately minimal — no gamification, no streaks, no social features. Just you, the cards, and an honest accuracy score.

Available on the App Store and Google Play.
