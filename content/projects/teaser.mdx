Teaser is a complete studio management platform for fitness professionals — workout builder, exercise vault, and class scheduler in one app.

The core product insight: fitness instructors spend hours per week assembling workouts in Notes or spreadsheets. Teaser replaces that with a structured, drag-and-drop curriculum builder designed around how instructors actually think.

## What's inside

- **Exercise Vault** — browse, filter, and tag hundreds of exercises by muscle group, equipment, and intensity
- **Workout Builder** — sequence exercises into structured blocks with reps, sets, rest periods, and coaching cues
- **Class Scheduler** — calendar-based management for assigning workouts to classes and tracking attendance

Available on the App Store and Google Play.
