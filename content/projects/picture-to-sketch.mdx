Picture to Sketch is a web app that gives artists a focused, distraction-free environment for photo reference practice.

The app pulls from a curated photography library via the Pexels API — you can search by subject or get a random selection. An optional timed display mode cycles through images at a set interval, mimicking gesture drawing sessions with real photographic subjects.

## The philosophy

Most online drawing tools push AI-generated references or gamified lesson plans. This is the opposite — a quiet, honest tool that gets out of your way so you can draw.

No accounts. No streak tracking. No AI. Just photos and a timer.
