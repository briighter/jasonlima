Canvas started as a performance experiment — could you build a whiteboard that
stays at 60fps regardless of element count?

The answer: yes, with a quadtree-based culling system and aggressive use of
`OffscreenCanvas` for worker thread rendering.

## What this project taught me

Building a collaborative editing tool forces you to confront the full complexity of
distributed state. CRDTs, operational transforms, conflict resolution — theory meets
practice fast when two cursors are fighting over the same element.
