A fully interactive design thinking curriculum inspired by Stanford d.school and IDEO methodology — built as a web experience rather than a slide deck.

The course walks through six phases: Tune, Empathize, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test. Each phase contains exercises pulled from real frameworks used by top design consultancies.

## What makes it different

Most design thinking resources are passive. This one is active — every phase has exercises you complete in the browser, a course map to jump between phases, and a final outcome: a business built on evidence, not assumption.

The goal was to make the methodology accessible to engineers and builders who learn by doing, not reading.
