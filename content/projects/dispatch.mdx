Dispatch is a purpose-built event routing layer designed to decouple producers
from consumers in high-throughput microservice environments.

The core insight: most event buses are over-engineered for 95% of use cases.
Dispatch is deliberately minimal — no schemas, no middleware, no DSL. Just
reliable delivery with backpressure support.

## Technical highlights

- Consumer groups with at-least-once delivery guarantees
- Dead letter queue with configurable retry backoff
- Prometheus metrics out of the box
- Single binary, zero external dependencies beyond Redis
